## Code reviews

**Please message me directly on Slack if you need a review.** I get a high volume of GitHub notifications daily and yours will likely get buried. A quick Slack message ensures I see it and can prioritize accordingly.

## Best times to reach me

- **Mornings (9am-12pm MT):** Usually in focus mode or meetings — Slack me but don't expect an instant reply
- **Afternoons (1pm-4pm MT):** Generally more available for ad-hoc chats and pairing
- **Thursdays:** I try to keep this as a low-meeting focus day. If it can wait, please schedule for another day

## How I prefer to communicate

- **Quick questions:** Slack DM or thread — don't wait for a meeting
- **Design decisions / architecture:** Start a Slack thread with context, then we can hop on a call if needed
- **Pairing sessions:** Schedule something on my calendar so I can plan around it — unplanned pairing tends to eat more time than expected for both of us
- **Non-urgent reviews:** Slack me a link with a one-liner on what to look for. I'll get to it within the day

## Meetings

- I try to keep meetings to their scheduled time. If we're going over, I'll suggest continuing async or scheduling a follow-up
- For 1:1s and syncs, having a quick agenda or topic list ahead of time helps us make the most of the time
- I'll decline meetings I can't add value to — don't take it personally
